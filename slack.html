How It Works

Four steps. One quarter.

Agents in Slack.

01
Pick & Match
Choose your campaign from 44+ agent blueprints. We assign a Solution Architect who's built this exact agent before on Salesforce.
02
SA Builds on Sandbox
Your SA constructs the Flow library, Agentforce configuration, and governance framework on a Salesforce sandbox. Topics, actions, guardrails, MCP connections — all set up before it touches Slack.
03
Deploy to Slack
The agent surfaces in your Slack workspace via the Slack Agent Kit. Your team @mentions it in-channel. It uses MCP and Real-Time Search to pull live CRM context. It shows its reasoning step by step — transparent, not a black box. The whole team sees what it does. That's progressive trust: the agent earns more responsibility over time.
04
Your Team Steers
Weekly office hours. The agent never acts without human confirmation on high-stakes decisions. Your team corrects it, steers it, and learns from it — together, in the channel. Performing agents carry over. Underperformers get retired.

Every engagement ships with: governance infrastructure (Testing Center, Observability, guardrails configured on your instance) · ROI attribution template per agent per campaign · agent fleet lifecycle view (active, carry-over, retired) · cost governance setup (Flex Credits / consumption monitoring) · full handoff documentation so any SA can continue the work.
